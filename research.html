Research & Methodology

Technical deep dive into the ESP32-based monitoring system and data collection processes.

Technology Stack

ESP32 Microcontroller

The heart of our system, providing WiFi connectivity and processing power for sensor data integration.

IoT Sensor Array

Specialized sensors for measuring pH level, Turbidity (NTU), Temperature (°C), and TDS (ppm) with high accuracy.

Web Analytics

Custom dashboard built with HTML5/CSS3 and JavaScript for real-time data visualization.

The Research Process

01

Sensor Calibration

Rigorous testing and calibration using standard solutions to ensure measurement accuracy across all units.

02

Data Acquisition

Continuous polling of sensor data by the ESP32, which is then processed and formatted for cloud transmission.

03

Analytics & Visualization

The web application retrieves data and performs trend analysis to identify variations in water quality over time.

04

Data Transmission

Implementing secure communication protocols to ensure reliable transfer of sensor readings to the database.

05

Performance Evaluation

Testing individual sensor modules under various environmental conditions to verify response time and precision.

06

System Deployment

Final implementation of monitoring units in campus locations to evaluate real-word safety and impact.

Monitored Parameters

pH

pH Value

Measures the acidity or alkalinity of the water. Crucial for ensuring the water is within the safe potable range of 6.5 to 8.5.

NTU

Turbidity

Measures the cloudiness or haziness caused by individual particles. Low turbidity is a key indicator of clean, high-quality water.

°C

Temperature

Monitors thermal variations which can affect the effectiveness of disinfection and the solubility of chemical constituents.

ppm

TDS

Total Dissolved Solids measures the combined content of all inorganic and organic substances contained in a liquid. It evaluates the overall water purity.
