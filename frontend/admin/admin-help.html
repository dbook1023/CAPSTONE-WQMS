Admin Task FAQs

How do I move or swap a physical sensor device to a different fountain?

Go to the Hardware Registry page and click Register Device. Enter the existing MAC address (Serial number) of the ESP32 unit and select the new destination fountain. The system will automatically unbind the device from its previous fountain and reassign it. All future water quality readings from that device will route to the new fountain, while historical data remains preserved.

How do I add a new user or administrator account?

Navigate to the User Management page and click Add User. Choose the account type — User (System Operator) or Administrator. Fill in the first name, last name, and email address. For user accounts, select an Engineering Branch (General, Pollution Control, Maintenance, or create a custom one) and assign a System Role: Operator (monitoring & alerts), Technician (hardware troubleshooting), or Viewer (read-only). New accounts are created with a default password of Password123!.

How do I add a new fountain or edit an existing one?

Open the Fountain Status Monitor page and click New Fountain. Enter a unique Fountain ID (e.g. F001), a name, a location description, and an initial status (Online, Warning, or Offline). To edit, click the Configure gear icon or the ⋮ menu on any fountain card and choose Edit. You can also toggle monitoring on/off per fountain or delete a fountain from the dropdown menu.

How do I resolve an alert or export alert logs?

Go to Alert Management. Each alert card shows the affected fountain, the parameter reading (pH, TDS, Turbidity, or Temperature), and a recommended maintenance action. Click the Resolve button on any alert to mark it as resolved. Use the filter tabs (All, Active, Critical, Warnings, Safe, Resolved) to narrow the list, and click the Export button to download the currently visible alerts as a CSV file.

How do I generate and download a water quality report?

Navigate to Reports & Analytics and click Generate Report (or use the Daily / Weekly / Monthly quick cards). In the modal, select the report timeframe, choose a specific fountain or all campus fountains, and pick an export format — PDF Document or Excel Spreadsheet (CSV). Click Generate & Download. You can also view, archive, or restore past reports from the Recent Reports list.

How do I change my admin profile or password?

Go to System Settings. Under the Profile tab you can update your full name, email, job title, phone number, and profile picture. Under the Security tab you can change your password (enter current password, then a new one), and toggle security policies including Two-Factor Authentication, Session Timeout, and Login Attempt Limiting.
