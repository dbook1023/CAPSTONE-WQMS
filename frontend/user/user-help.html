Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the water quality data updated in the portal?

Water quality telemetry is transmitted from our ESP32 sensor nodes every 5 minutes. The Live Monitoring page automatically updates in real-time to display the latest pH, TDS, Turbidity, and Temperature metrics.

What are the acceptable safety thresholds for drinking water?

According to the Philippine National Standards for Drinking Water (PNSDW) enforced by our system:

  • pH Level: 6.5 to 8.5 pH (Acidity / Alkalinity)
  • Turbidity: ≤ 5.0 NTU (Water clarity)
  • Total Dissolved Solids (TDS): ≤ 500 ppm (Dissolved minerals)
  • Temperature: 15°C to 30°C
What do the different fountain status indicators mean?

On the Fountain Status page:

  • Online / Active: All parameters are within safe drinking water standards.
  • Warning: One or more parameters are approaching threshold limits; close monitoring is active.
  • Offline / Maintenance: The fountain unit is disconnected or undergoing physical maintenance.
How can I view or export water quality reports?

Navigate to the Reports section. You can view overall system compliance rates, filter reports by date range or fountain location, and inspect historical water quality summaries.

How do I update my profile or change my account password?

Go to Account Settings. You can update your profile name, email, phone number, and Engineering Branch information, as well as change your account password under the Security section.

What should I do if a drinking fountain shows a Warning or Offline status?

If a drinking fountain is flagged with a Warning or Offline status, automated alerts are dispatched to technical staff. If you notice physical water clarity issues, refrain from using the fountain and notify your campus site administrator or facilities office immediately.
