About AquaMonitor

Ensuring sustainable and safe drinking water for the OLFU community through IoT innovation.

Our Project

AquaMonitor is a state-of-the-art Water Quality Monitoring System developed as a Capstone project at Our Lady of Fatima University. Our system utilizes ESP32 microcontrollers and high-precision sensors to monitor critical water parameters in real-time.

By integrating hardware with advanced web analytics, we provide transparent and accessible data regarding the quality of drinking water across the campus fountains.

The Mission

To promote health and safety by ensuring water quality compliance with national standards.

To provide real-time data transparency to the university administration and student body.

To leverage IoT technology for proactive maintenance of drinking water facilities.

Institutional Partnership
